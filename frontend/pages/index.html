The Season's Selection

Curated by Executive Chef Julian Vance, these signature creations define our commitment to culinary perfection.

Chicken Kosha

A rich, slow-cooked Bengali-style chicken curry featuring a thick, dark-brown spice paste rather than a runny gravy. It is characterized by tender chicken, often with potatoes, marinated in yogurt and cooked in mustard oil to achieve a deep, aromatic flavor.

Muton Biryani

A rich, aromatic Indian rice dish featuring tender, marinated mutton (goat meat) slow-cooked with basmati rice, caramelized onions, yogurt, ghee, and exotic spices.

Butter Paneer

A popular, mildly spiced North Indian curry. It features soft paneer cubes cooked in a creamy, velvety, and slightly sweet tomato-cashew gravy enriched with butter, ginger, and aromatic spices like kasuri methi.

Our Legacy

The Heritage of
Khai Khai

Rooted in the rich traditions of Indian culinary heritage, Khai Khai celebrates the timeless flavors passed down through generations. From the royal kitchens of Rajasthan to the coastal spices of Kerala, every dish reflects a journey across India’s diverse culture.

We source our ingredients from local farmers and traditional artisans, ensuring authenticity, sustainability, and unmatched quality. Our kitchen blends age-old techniques with modern refinement, where every meal is crafted with precision, passion, and a deep respect for Indian gastronomy.
